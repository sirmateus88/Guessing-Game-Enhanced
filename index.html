The year is 2050

The Quizler (totally not a Riddler rip off) has just planted devices of mass annoyance in every home in Gotham. The devices play a beeping sound every 15 minutes until someone starts looking for them; then, silence. While the citizens are going crazy, there is one way to stop these devices, to play the guessing game.

To play the game, you have 5 chances to guess a number between 1 and 100. Get it right, and the devices all turn off. Get it wrong, and a second device activates that keeps the temperature always slightly too cold in a room. The government has called upon you, a 7 time lottery winner, for your outstanding guessing skills.

You are our only hope!!
